Some of the words in mental health are signals to act. [Suicidal ideation](/terms/suicidal-ideation/) and [self-harm](/terms/self-harm/) are the clearest, and they deserve attention the same day, not eventually. The heavy feelings of [hopelessness](/terms/hopelessness/) and [worthlessness](/terms/worthlessness/) often travel with them.

Other words point to a possible loss of contact with reality: [hallucination](/terms/hallucination/), [delusion](/terms/delusion/), [paranoia](/terms/paranoia/), and the elevated, sometimes dangerous state of [mania](/terms/mania/). Severe [agitation](/terms/agitation/) can be urgent too. These can be part of a psychiatric emergency.

If you or someone you know is in crisis, call or text 988 in the United States to reach the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline, call 911, or go to the nearest emergency room. See our <a href="/crisis-resources/">crisis resources</a> for more.
